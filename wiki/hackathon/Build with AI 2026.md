# Build with AI 2026

**The event we are here to win.** *Build with AI Wrocław: Architecting the Future
of Products* — a 6-day GDG Wrocław intensive that turns UI/UX designers, developers
and DevOps engineers into cross-functional Product Creators, using AI across the
whole SDLC. It ends in a judged Grand Hackathon.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Organizer | GDG Wrocław |
| Dates | **29 Jun – 4 Jul 2026** (2–6 PM UTC daily) |
| Venue | Uniwersytet Ekonomiczny we Wrocławiu, 118/120 Komandorska, 53-345 |
| Teams | cross-functional: UI/UX + Developer + DevOps |
| Judges | Adrian Romański, Dawid Perdek, Marek Mysior — see [[People]] |
| Source | gdg.community.dev event page (scraped 2026-07-02) |

## 📅 Day-by-day agenda
Each day = a 20-pt pillar in the [[Judging Criteria]].

- **Day 1 · 29 Jun — Product Design, Scope & MVP.** Persona, problem statement,
  MVP scope. IT lifecycle, Design Thinking, SDLC, BPMN, Event Storming, Kanban.
  *Trainers:* Dawid Perdek, Natalia Kierczuk, Artur Suchwalko.
- **Day 2 · 30 Jun — UI/UX & AI-Native Design Systems.** Digital accessibility,
  AI-powered design systems, designs→products via **MCP servers**.
  *Trainers:* Kinga Witko, Witold Iglewski, Przemysław Nowak.
- **Day 3 · 1 Jul — Scaling Frontend Architecture & AI-Assisted Debugging.**
  **Nx** monorepos, modern **Angular** (signals), **ng-diagram**, Chrome DevTools MCP.
  *Trainers:* Julia Rapczyńska, Adrian Romański, Jakub Kubacki, Dominika Zając.
- **Day 4 · 2 Jul — Fullstack, Architecture & LLM Engineering.** Angular↔**NestJS**,
  **Docker** + **Sequelize**, **evaluation scoreboards**, autonomous agentic loops.
  *Trainers:* Greg Radzio, Adam Kowalski.
- **Day 5 · 3 Jul — Deployment, Scale & Optimization.** MCP servers, `pytriz`,
  deploy to **Google Cloud** with **CI/CD**. *Trainers:* Marek Mysior, Moksh
  Atukuri, Jacek Kubiak.
- **Day 6 · 4 Jul — 🏆 Grand Hackathon.** Cross-functional teams design, build and
  launch a complete product. The judged finale.

The daily topics define the [[Tech Stack]].
