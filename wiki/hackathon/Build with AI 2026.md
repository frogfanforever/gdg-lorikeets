# Build with AI 2026

The event we're here to win: **Build with AI Wrocław: Architecting the Future of
Products** — a 6-day GDG Wrocław intensive that turns designers/devs/DevOps into
cross-functional Product Creators, ending in a judged Grand Hackathon.

| | |
|---|---|
| Organizer | GDG Wrocław |
| Dates | **29 Jun – 4 Jul 2026** (2–6 PM UTC daily) |
| Venue | Uniwersytet Ekonomiczny we Wrocławiu, 118/120 Komandorska |
| Teams | cross-functional: UI/UX + Developer + DevOps |
| Judges | see [[People]] |
| Source | `ai/context/hackathon/build-with-ai-2026.md` |

## The 6 days (each = a 20-pt pillar in [[Judging Criteria]])
1. **29 Jun** — Product Design, Scope & MVP
2. **30 Jun** — UI/UX & A11Y (AI-native design systems)
3. **1 Jul** — Scaling Frontend Architecture & AI-Assisted Debugging (Nx, Angular)
4. **2 Jul** — Fullstack, Architecture & LLM Engineering (NestJS, agentic loops)
5. **3 Jul** — Deployment, Scale & Optimization (MCP, Cloud Run, CI/CD)
6. **4 Jul** — 🏆 Grand Hackathon

The daily topics define the [[Tech Stack]] and map 1:1 to the [[Judging Criteria]].
