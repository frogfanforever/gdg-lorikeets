# Winning Playbook

How Team Lorikeets take first at [[Build with AI 2026]]. Source:
`ai/context/hackathon/winning-playbook.md`, confirmed against the [[Judging Criteria]].

## The core bet
**The judges are the trainers** ([[People]]) and points split evenly across their
days. Reward recognition of what each taught; balance across all 5 pillars.

## Land all three judge pillars
- **Perdek (Day 1):** open with persona + problem + scoped MVP; show the BPMN /
  Event Storming artifact.
- **Romański (Day 3):** clean Nx monorepo, signal-based Angular, ng-diagram used
  for real. Provable via [[WCS]].
- **Mysior (Day 5):** deploy live to Cloud Run via CI/CD. A public URL beats
  localhost every time.

## Differentiators (map to taught material)
- **[[Criterion Zero]] first** — a working product that solves the assigned task.
- **Evaluation scoreboard** on screen — most teams hand-wave AI quality; we
  measure it ([[Eval Suite]], [[WCS]], [[API Acceptance Eval]]).
- **A11Y pass** (Day 2, 20 pts) — cheap, high-yield, few bother.
- **Real deployment** (Day 5, 20 pts) — deploy early, redeploy often.

## Our edge
The repo already ships a `.jsonl` ingestion pipeline ([[Discord Integration]]) and
an eval scoreboard — a ready-made data source + eval substrate for the LLM pillar.
