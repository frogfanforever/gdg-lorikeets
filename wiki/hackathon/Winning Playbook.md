# Winning Playbook

How Team Lorikeets take first at [[Build with AI 2026]].

> 📄 **Canonical:** `ai/context/hackathon/winning-playbook.md` (full plan +
> time-boxed Grand Hackathon schedule).

- **Core bet:** the judges are the trainers ([[People]]) and points split evenly —
  balance across all 5 pillars, land each judge's day.
- **[[Criterion Zero]] first** — a working product that solves the assigned task.
- **Differentiators:** an on-screen **evaluation scoreboard** ([[Eval Suite]],
  [[WCS]], [[API Acceptance Eval]]), an **A11Y** pass (Day 2), and a **live Cloud Run**
  deploy (Day 5) — each a full 20% most teams under-invest in.
- **Our edge:** the repo already ships a `.jsonl` pipeline ([[Discord Integration]])
  + eval scoreboard — a ready-made data source and eval substrate.
