# Judging Criteria

The **official rubric**, scraped from the hackathon Discord `#ewaluacja-punktów`
(posted by judge Adrian Romański, 30 Jun). Source:
`ai/context/hackathon/judging-criteria.md`. The panel judges as **Client +

## [[Criterion Zero]] — the gate
The app **must fulfil its assigned domain task** with correct, sensible AI output.
Fail this and the project is disqualified *before* points are counted.
