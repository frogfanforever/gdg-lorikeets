# Build with AI Wrocław: Architecting the Future of Products

**The event we are here to win.** A 6-day intensive that turns UI/UX designers,
developers and DevOps engineers into cross-functional Product Creators, using AI
as the connective tissue across the whole SDLC. It ends in a Grand Hackathon.

## 📅 Day-by-day agenda

### Day 1 — 29 Jun · Product Design, Scope & MVP
Go from a blank page to a scoped MVP you keep building all week. Define a real
user persona and a real problem statement; map the core flow; set MVP scope.
Covers: IT project lifecycle, Design Thinking, SDLC, BPMN, Event Storming, Kanban.
**Trainers:** Dawid Perdek, Natalia Kierczuk, Artur Suchwalko.

### Day 2 — 30 Jun · UI/UX & AI-Native Design Systems
Digital accessibility, AI-powered design systems, and turning designs into
working products via **MCP servers**.
**Trainers:** Kinga Witko, Witold Iglewski, Przemyslaw Nowak.

### Day 3 — 1 Jul · Scaling Frontend Architecture & AI-Assisted Debugging
**Nx monorepos**, modern **Angular** (signals), native diagramming with
**ng-diagram**, and AI-powered debugging (Chrome DevTools MCP).
**Trainers:** Julia Rapczynska, Adrian Romanski, Jakub Kubacki, Dominika Zając.

### Day 4 — 2 Jul · Fullstack, Architecture & LLM Engineering  ⬅️ TODAY
Connect **Angular** frontends to **NestJS** backends; **Docker** + **Sequelize**;
**evaluation scoreboards**; and **autonomous agentic loops**.
**Trainers:** Greg Radzio, Adam Kowalski.

### Day 5 — 3 Jul · Deployment, Scale & Optimization
Building **MCP servers**, integrating the **pytriz** package for technical
problem-solving, and deploying to **Google Cloud** with **CI/CD**.
**Trainers:** Marek Mysior, Moksh Atukuri, Jacek Kubiak.

### Day 6 — 4 Jul · 🏆 Grand Hackathon
Cross-functional teams design, build, and launch a complete product integrating
all components from the week. This is the judged finale.
